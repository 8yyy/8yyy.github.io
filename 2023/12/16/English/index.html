第一章：词性及句子成分

句子成分

句子=主语(n)+谓语(v)

n：名词noun v：动词verb

1.1 名词

名词短语

名词短语=限定词+修饰词(adj)+主体词(n)

例如 a good boy

限定词构成：

  1. 冠词 a/an the
  2. 形容词性物主代词 my his
  3. 名词所有格 one’s Tom’s
  4. 指示代词 this that these those
  5. 不定代词 some every another
  6. 基数词 one two three
  7. 疑问代词 what which

以book/books为例

a book. the books.

my book. my books.

Tom’s book. Tom’s books.

That book. Those books.

evert book. another book. some books.

one book. two books.

what book. what books.

可省略

限定词 修饰词 主体词
a good boy
a boy
1 good boys
1 boys
2 good news
2 news
3 China
3 Tom
the rich 笼统(people)
the best 明显(student)
these (books)

1.Cs 可数名词复数

2.u 不可数名词

3.专用名词(也是不可数名词)

4.很笼统明显的时候可以省略主体词，例如the rich (people). the bset (student).these (books).

可数名词 vs 不可数名词

  • 可数
    • boy
    • girl
    • apple
    • tree
    • book
    • …
  • 不可数
    • water
    • air
    • money
    • …
  • 既是可数又是不可数
    • chicken
      • 小鸡，可数，复数形式是chickens
      • 鸡肉，不可数
    • fish
      • 鱼，可数，复数形式是fish
      • 鱼肉，不可数
    • …

可数分为单数和复数，复数分为规则和不规则

不可数

  • 不管

  • 量化

    • a glass of water
    • a piece of paper

  • 可数化

    • a good time（一段时间量化）

代词

人称代词

主格 宾格 形物代 名物代 反身代

2 You you your yours yourself
3 He him his his himself

复数

主格 宾格 形物代 名物代 反身代
1 We us our ours ourselves
2 You you your yours yourselves
3 They them their theirs themselves

主格做主语(S)

eg: I like English.

宾格做宾语(o)或表语(p)

eg: I hate him. him宾语

eg: It is me. me表语

形物代(adj)+名词(n)=名物代

eg: This is my book.

eg: This book is mine.

反身代先得有该人称

eg: I love her. 正确

eg: I love myself. 正确

eg: I love herself. 错误

指示代词

  • 近一点的东西
    • 单数，This
    • 复数，These
  • 远一点的东西
    • 单数，That 可数单数，不可数名词
    • 复数，Those 可数复数

The weater of CD is cooler than that(the weater) of CQ.

The oranges of HN are sweeter than those(the oranges) of HB.

  • both两者都 either两者之一 neither两者都不 all三者及以上 none三者及以上都不
  • many很多 much很多 more更多 most最多

  • other others another the other the others

  • 复合不定代词 every some any no * body thing one 12个

疑问代词

who is he? he is who.

what do you want? you want what.

不定代词之other

(限,adj,pron)other+n(可数名词复数)=others

others后不能加名词

eg. I have other. 错误

eg. I have others. 正确

eg. I have other plans. 正确

eg. I have others plans. 错误 others等价于other plans

一般情况 other+可数名词复数

no/any/every+other+可数名词单数/不可数名词

others泛指其他人/事/物

eg. Some students…;other students…;others(other students)…;others(other students)….

others特指剩余的全部

明确有总数

数词

一般分为两种

  • 基数词
    • one,two,three

分为四种

a.整数 1,2,3

b.小数 1.1,1.2,1.3

c.分数 分子用基数，分母用序数（分子>1，分母变复数）

eg. 1/3 one third 2/3 two thirds

d.百分数 100%

  • 序数词
    • first,second,third

基数词可以做限定词，而序数词倾向于形容词

不定式、动名词和句子

不定式 To+动词原形构成的短语=不定式短语

eg. To learn English well.

动名词

eg. Smoking.

句子

eg. You are a pig.

以上三者特点：

  1. 作主语看作单数（一件事看成一个整体）

To learn English well is very important.

eg.Smoking is bad.

That you are a pig is a fact.

  1. “头重脚轻”时，用It做形式主语/形式宾语

It is very important to learn English well.

It is a fact that you are a pig.

it是形式主语，而to learn English well和that you are a pig是真正的主语

判断主语

During the 1990s,American country music has become more and more popular.

名词短语做主语

we often speak English in calss.

人称代词做主语

One-third of the students in this class are girls.

数词做主语，A of B = B的A其中B是修饰词，也就是说，of后面的B是用来缩小A的范围的，也就是说A才是主语

To swim in the river is a great pleasure.

不定式短语做主语

Smoking does harm to the health.

动名词作主语

The rich should help the poor.

名词短语作主语

When we are going to have an English test has not been decided.

句子做主语，主从

It is necessary to master a foreign language

不定式短语做主语

That he isn’t at home is not true.

句子作主语，主从

笼统的名词短语，代词，数次，找到将他变小的东西，就是定语

1.2 形容词与定语

定语：修饰名词

词性：形容词性

定语将一个名词从大变小（从笼统逐渐具体）

eg. One-third of the students in this class are girls.

of the students 是 One-third 的定语

in this class 是 the students 的定语

主干：One-third are girls.

定语分类

  • 前置定语 [单个，独立]
    • a good boy
  • 后置定语（定语后置）[短语句子]
    • a boy under the tree

定语构成

副词（adv）用作定语

People (living/being) there are very friendly.

He didn’t like the man (living)  downstairs.

做了省略可以用副词作定语

形容词短语用作定语

The next man is a scientist.

The man next to me is a scientist.

介词短语用作定语

The boy under the tree is Tom.

The tallest boy in our class is John.

现在分词短语、过去分词短语、动词不定式作定语

I have something to say.(to say 将 somthing 范围减小)

The boy crying over there is my classmate.

The house built last year is impressive.

判断定语

The letter on the desk is for Mr. Wu.

介词短语用作定语

The woman with a baby in her arms is his mother.

We need a place twice larger than this one.

She carried a basket full of eggs.

lt’s a book worth no more than one dollar.

lt’s a city far from the coast.

He has money enough to buy a car.

The man downstairs was trying to sleep.

副词用作定语，省略了living

There are lots of places of interest needing repairing in our city. 错误

There are lots of places of interest needing repairing in our city. 正确

places of interest是名词短语，风景名胜

Tigers belonging to meat-eating animals feed on meat.

A boy calling himself John wanted to see you.

He picked up a wallet lying on the ground on the way back home. 错误

He picked up a wallet lying on the ground on the way back home. 正确

There are many clothes to be washed.

Most of the singers invited to the party were from America. 错误

Most of the singers invited to the party were from America. 正确

类似于1/3，Most也是很笼统的，用of the singers将其变小

He still remember the day when he first stepped into the Old Trafford.

1.3 动词

动词可以构成谓语和非谓语

谓语一定由动词构成

动词分类

1.助动词 辅助 [不能单独构成谓语]

  • Be动词 am is are was were be

  • 情态动词

    • can could
    • will would
    • shall should
    • may might
    • must have to
    • need dare
    • had better（上面是一组，这是一个）

  1. 不单独构成谓语

  2. +do

  3. 无人称变化

  4. 有本事含义

  • 侠义助动词 do dode did

  • 完成助动词 have has had

2.实义动词 行为动词 [表具体的动作：外部动作和内部动作]

  • 及物动词 vt+宾语(o) 主谓宾结构svo eg: I love you. 动宾
  • 不及物动词 vi
    • 不+o => 主谓结构sv eg: Time files.
    • +介+o => 主谓宾结构svo eg: I look at the man. 介at宾the man

3.系动词 +表语[表示主语是什么或怎么样] [不表具体动作] He is a student.

  • 表状态 Be,stay,keep,remain,lie,stand,exist
  • 表感官 feel,smell,taste,look,sound
  • 表变化 become,go,get,turn,fall
  • 表像 seem,appear

动词分类问题

Be可以是助动词，也可以是系动词

助

  • +doing 进行 she is crying. crying是实义动词，is是助动词
  • +done 被动 he is killed. killed是实义动词，is是助动词

系：

  • I am a man. 表示是

He is a student. 系动词，他是一个学生，is表示是

He is playing football. 助动词，他在踢足球，playing是实义动词

His hobby is playing football. 系动词，他的爱好是踢足球，is表示是

He is asked to do this job. 助动词，他被叫去做这个工作，asked是实义动词

判断助/系 or 实义

助动词 vs 实义动词

He did it. 实义动词，他做了这件事，did表示做

He did like it. 助动词，他确实喜欢它，like是实义动词

He did do it. 助动词，他确实做，do是实义动词

I have a dream. 实义动词

I have finished my homework. 助动词，finished是实义动词

I have had supper. 助动词，我已经吃了晚饭，had是实义动词

系动词 vs 实义动词

He goes to school by bike. 实义动词

The food goes bad. 系动词，食物变坏了，goes表示变化

He turns around. 实义动词

The leaf turns yellow. 系动词，树叶变黄了，turns表示变化

及物动词vi vs 不及物动词vt

The door opened. vi，主谓

He opened the door. vt，+宾语the door，主谓宾

The meeting began at six. vi，+介词at，主谓

We began the meeting at six. vt，+宾语the meeting，主谓宾

The man walked away. vi，主谓

He walked the dog every day. vt，+宾语the dog，主谓宾

She washes clothes at home. vt，主谓宾

The clothes washes well. vi，主谓

He listens to the music every day. vi，+介词to，主谓宾

The baby stopped crying when he saw his mother. vt，动名词crying作宾语
